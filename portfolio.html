1. Responsive website

This project was dedicated to a certain art style that has been assigned to me. The purpose of this website was to provide information about the art style, Arts & Crafts Movement, and me as a creator. The proces concerned creating a passion collage and arguing my decisions about design in a project documentation. Building a responsive website from scratch using css and html was a great challenge since I have never done this before.

2. Data collection

Learning about different kinds of research was the main topic of this assigment. In order to properly understand particular method of data collection, different excersises were held. For instance observing people during commute or while waiting for a bus or a train. I also learned how to ask questions in person during an interview or how to create clear questions for a survey.

3. Prototype

Before coming to Denmark all of us, international students, had to look up all the important information. This was often a horibly hard thing to do because most of the important web sites are in danish. Many information is 'hidden' in the content and is hard to find. Therefore, in this project we decided to fix all the problems that made moving to Denmark hard for us. We approached this assigment by combining our knowledge of basic UX and basic web and mostly our personal experience. One of our main goals was to make this page not only usable but also useful.

4. Design sprint

During the days of DESIGN SPRINT assigment we learned how to approach an idea step-by-step. As a group we were 'forced' to cooperate and maintain a friendly enviroment in order to reach our common goal. Even though we all started of with different ideas, at the end of the project we were all sattisfied and proud of our group.

5. Character design & animation

My favorite assigment would have to be this one - character design. The characters are based on real-life people, me and my boyfriend and the cat is our cat Joey. I was also inspired by many instagram accounts that have a similar topic. I mention all my inspiration and progress in the project documentation.

6. Java Script controlled animation

For me the most challenging project of all in the first semester was definitely this one. Combining JS, html and css was something I had to spent a lot of time on.

7. Simple calculator

8. Game

Creating an educative game for new students that are coming to Denmark was so much fun. This was a great opportunity to look back at everything that we have faced without knowing what we were doing. For me, illustrating and creating the visuals of this game was a dream role in the team. The main goal of the game is to read about the essentials of moving to Denmark and then testing your knowledge in a fun quiz which you can retake as many times as you wish.

9. Food Sharing Copenhagen

The making of a campaign for Food Sharing Copenhagen was a complex process of using almost everything that we have learned during the first semester. From creating and running a survey to the actual making of a video implemented in a campaign site. All of this was a challenge but looking at the result was a true satisfaction.
